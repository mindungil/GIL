# Code of Conduct

The gil project follows the [Contributor Covenant 2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/) for all participation in our spaces (issues, pull requests, discussions, chat).

## Scope

This applies to all project spaces and to anyone representing the project in public.

## Enforcement

Concerns may be reported to the maintainers at `coc@jedutools.io`. Reports are handled privately. Maintainers may take any action they consider appropriate, up to and including a permanent ban from project spaces.

For the full text of expectations and enforcement guidelines, see the [canonical Contributor Covenant 2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/).
